About Chidren Camp

Whilst each of our camps is unique in its own way, we maintain several core values they all share - regardless of location, activity or age group. It's what makes them Scripture Union Holidays.
God
At the root all that we offer is our passion to give young people the opportunity to explore the Bible, learn more about God and discover his relevance to them today. So every holiday will devote part of each day to looking at the Bible together. How this is done will vary depending on the age group and facilities available. But we ensure all teaching is engaging and relevant and gives the opportunity to respond in an appropriate way.
Fun
Holidays are all about fun! And fun can be defined in many different ways. That's why our camps are a mixture of games, craft, sports and excursions. Attendees are welcome to do activities they already love or challenge themselves with new ones. Of course, there will be lots of opportunities to make new friends. Common to all of them is the Fun-Factor!
