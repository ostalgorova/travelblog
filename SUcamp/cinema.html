About Cinemaclub

We invite high school and university students to watch and discuss movie with us! Our club is open for everyone. We will show you world famous classic films and best films of our century. All of these films made in different countries tell us about problems, which are actual for young people: relationship between men and women, relationship between generations, loneliness and friendship.
Cinemaclub is totally free! It is held every second Saturday, 17:00. (You can find a map in the contact page.)
